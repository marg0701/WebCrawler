News' Recommendation System

In this app we can recommend you daily news about a topic of your interest based on the model LDA. The news are taken from:

  • El País
  • Excelsior
  • CNN Español

You can access to the main pages in the 'Link' section.

In 'Recomendation' section you get the recommendations of the news about the topic of your interest

In 'Results' section you can see the general results of the model LDA
